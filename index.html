About

Maverick, the ultimate 80s and 90s function band, is guaranteed to get you dancing! Featuring guitar, keys, keytar, bass, drums, and dynamic vocals, our talented lineup delivers electrifying performances for any event. Specializing in the best hits from the 80s and 90s, Maverick brings an energetic and nostalgic vibe that will keep your guests entertained and the dance floor packed all night long.
With years of experience and a passion for live music, each member of Maverick brings their unique flair and expertise to the stage. From classic rock anthems to pop hits and everything in between, our diverse setlist ensures there's something for everyone. Whether you're hosting a wedding, corporate event, birthday party, or any celebration, Maverick creates an unforgettable atmosphere that will make your event a night to remember.Let Maverick turn your event into a high-energy, memorable experience with the best music from the 80s and 90s!
